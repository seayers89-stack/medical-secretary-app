Bonus — runs on your PC

Task Tracker

A small offline task timer built for the middle of a working day. It's a separate desktop app you download — not part of your Veyn dashboard — so your data stays on your own computer, not on Veyn's servers.

What it does:

  • Time what you're working on — start, pause, and mark done with one click
  • Set an optional time budget per task and get an alert when it runs over
  • Tag tasks (billing, letters, admin…) and see where your time actually goes, by day, week, or month
  • Repeating tasks — daily, weekly, or on specific dates — appear automatically each day
  • Works entirely offline, with nothing sent to Veyn
