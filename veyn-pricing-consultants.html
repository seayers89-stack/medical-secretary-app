Why pay per contact, not a flat fee

We'd rather earn a small amount when you actually reach out than charge a flat fee whether you find anyone or not. If you're hiring at volume, a pass works out cheaper than paying per contact past roughly six or seven unlocks — pick whichever fits how often you're actually hiring.
