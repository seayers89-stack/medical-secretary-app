Payment data

When you make a purchase, payment is processed by Stripe. Veyn never sees or stores your card number or full payment details. Stripe provides us with a transaction reference, the amount paid, and the date — used to verify your access and process refunds. These records are retained for 7 years to comply with HMRC financial record-keeping requirements.

Support messages

If you contact us through the in-platform support feature, we store your messages to allow us to respond and track resolution. Support conversations are retained for as long as they may reasonably be needed to resolve the matter.

2. What we do not collect

Patient data is strictly prohibited on Veyn. Veyn is a professional directory — it is not a clinical system. No patient names, dates of birth, NHS numbers, diagnoses, treatment details, or any other patient-identifiable information must ever be entered into Veyn. Uploading or sharing patient data through the platform is a breach of these Terms, UK GDPR, and potentially the Data Protection Act 2018. If you believe patient data has been shared on the platform, contact us immediately at infoveynuk@gmail.com.
  • We do not collect, store, or process patient data of any kind.
  • We do not collect health data about users themselves.
  • We do not run advertising or analytics tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not collect your IP address or device fingerprint for profiling purposes.

4. How long we keep your data

When you delete your account, your personal data — including your profile, messages, and all associated records — is permanently and immediately deleted from our systems. This deletion cascades automatically across all related data.

The only exception is payment transaction records (amount, date, reference) which are retained for 7 years as required by HMRC.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed by our authentication provider (Supabase) and never stored in plain text.
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • Database access is protected by row-level security — users can only access their own data.
  • We use Stripe for payments so card data never touches our servers.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at infoveynuk@gmail.com.

Data breach notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours of becoming aware of it, as required by UK GDPR Article 33. Where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will also notify affected users without undue delay, and in any case within 72 hours where feasible, in accordance with UK GDPR Article 34. Notification will be sent to the email address on your account and will describe the nature of the breach, the data affected, and the steps we are taking.

7. Email communications

We send transactional emails that are necessary for the operation of your account — for example, email confirmation, password reset, and notifications about contact unlock requests or refunds. These are sent on the legal basis of contract performance and cannot be opted out of while you hold an account, as they are essential to service delivery.

We do not send marketing or promotional emails without your prior opt-in consent. If we introduce any marketing communications in future, you will be asked to opt in explicitly and will always have a one-click unsubscribe option in every email.

To stop all email communications from Veyn, you can delete your account from your profile settings — this permanently removes your email address from our systems.
