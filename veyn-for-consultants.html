Searching outside London or Milton Keynes?

We'd rather tell you now than after you've searched and found nothing: our deepest pool today is in London and Milton Keynes, weighted toward general surgery, orthopaedics, and urology. Secretaries from anywhere can join Veyn, so coverage elsewhere is growing — but we're not going to imply a national pool we don't have yet. If you're hiring outside these regions or specialties, expect a thinner search today, with depth building as we grow.
