Precise, on purpose

What this is — and isn't

A registration check is a specific, useful thing. Here's exactly what it covers.

This is

  • ✓ Confirmation you hold a real, current GMC number
  • ✓ Required before you can message any secretary
  • ✓ A one-time check tied to your account, not repeated per message
  • ✓ The same trust signal every secretary sees, every time

This isn't

  • ✕ A full background, DBS, or fitness-to-practise check
  • ✕ A review of your specific clinic, practice, or employment history
  • ✕ Something you need to repeat each time you search or message
  • ✕ A paid service — there's no fee for this check

Why this, specifically

Secretaries can't see who's behind a message until they've already replied — on most platforms. Confirming your GMC number before you can send a single message means every secretary you contact already knows exactly who they're talking to, the same confidence document verification gives you about them.
