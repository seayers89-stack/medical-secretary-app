Where to get your DBS check

The gov.uk Basic check service is the default route for most secretarial roles (from £21.50). If you're self-employed and need a Standard or Enhanced check, a registered DBS Umbrella Body can process this directly — a route that's only been available since 21 January 2026. Once you have a certificate, upload it for free document verification on your profile.
